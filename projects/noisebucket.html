What is citizen science?

The collection and analysis of data relating to the natural world by members of the general public, typically as part of a collaborative project with professional scientists.

Citizen science (CS) (also known as crowd science, crowd-sourced science, civic science, volunteer monitoring or networked science) is scientific research conducted, in whole or in part, by amateur or nonprofessional scientists.

No academic background required. The participation of nonscientists in the process of gathering data according to specific scientific protocols and in the process of using and interpreting that data.

Research and Analysis

We spent more time and energy on figuring out what type of data the scholars need and how can we receive and retrieve these data. The app here is designed for two type of stakeholders, scholars and general public who do not have scientific professional background.

Scholars need data of the noise level and people's reflection. The precise data is the key element here. Also, since each indivdual might reflect each level of noise differently, so receive the data of how people feel about the noise level is also very important here.

General public care more about the rewards and the easiness of collecting data.General public, who do not have background of scientific knowledge here, are using the app for the rewards. The app also need to ease the process of data collecting.

Brainstorming

We conducted several different session of brainstorming. The first session is five minutes of brainstorming of the rewards, then 5 minutes of ways to retrieve the data, then 5 minutes of general features for both scholars and also general publics.

Scientific scholars. We figured that two important features are data of noise level and people's reflection of emotions. So these two types of data would be designed in the application.

General users. Rewards, in these case, are designed as giftcards. As users who go to Starbucks, Starbucks giftcards is considered as a very attractive rewards.
